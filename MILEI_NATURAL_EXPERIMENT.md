## III. TIPO A → TIPO B TRANSITION MECHANICS

### A. Before Milei (Tipo A: Kirchnerism 2003–2023)

**Structure**:
1. **Federal Government** (Casa Rosada) → Ministerio de Desarrollo Social
2. **Unidades de Gestión** (300+) controlled by:
   - Movimientos sociales (e.g., Movimiento Evita, Barrios de Pie, Polo Obrero)
   - Cooperativas de trabajo (worker cooperatives)
   - Municipios (peronist intendentes)
3. **Punteros/Referentes** → 1.2M beneficiaries (Potenciar Trabajo)

**Payoff Structure**:
- **Beneficiary**: ARS ~78k/month (2023) for 6 hrs/day "work" (often fictitious)
- **Puntero**: Control over ~100–500 beneficiaries each → electoral mobilization (rallies, piquetes, protests)
- **Movimiento Social**: Receive ~10–20% administrative overhead (government estimates) = **USD 34M/year nationwide**
- **Federal Government** (Kirchner): Mobilize 1.2M beneficiaries for protests → political leverage

**Pagni Confirmation** (p. 324): "Cada puntero ayuda, en promedio, a unas 85 personas. Cerca de dos tercios tienen un sueldo municipal o reciben un plan social."

**Type A Features**:
- ✓ Mediators (punteros)
- ✓ Personalized loyalty (client → puntero → party)
- ✓ Territorial control (piquetes block roads → punteros control streets)
- ✓ Low cost/voter (USD 10–50/voter after subtracting puntero overhead)

---

### B. After Milei (Tipo B: 2024–Present)

**Structure**:
1. **Federal Government** (Casa Rosada) → Ministerio de Capital Humano (Sandra Pettovello)
2. **ANSES** (social security agency) → direct digital transfers
3. **0800 helpline + online portal** → no physical offices
4. **Beneficiaries** receive direct bank deposits (no puntero intermediation)

**New Programs**:
1. **Volver al Trabajo** (Back to Work): ARS 66,000/month (~USD 65–70 at 2025 rates) direct deposit
   - **Eligibility**: Ex-Potenciar Trabajo beneficiaries (age 50+ or with disabilities)
   - **No contraprestación** (no work requirement) = pure cash transfer
   - **No puntero**: ANSES verifies eligibility via database

2. **Tarjeta Alimentar** (Food Card): Increased amounts, direct ANSES deposit to prepaid card
   - **No cooperativas**: Cards loaded automatically per beneficiary (mothers with children <14 years)
   - **No puntero distribution**: Banks/ATMs issue cards directly

**Payoff Structure**:
- **Beneficiary**: ARS 66k/month × 12 = ARS 792k/year ≈ **USD 800–900/year** (higher than Potenciar Trabajo ARS 78k/month = USD ~900/year)
- **Puntero**: **ELIMINATED** (no intermediation = no overhead)
- **Federal Government**: **Lower cost** (USD 39M/year savings from puntero overhead) + **no mobilization capacity** (beneficiaries not organized)

**Type B Features**:
- ✓ No mediators (direct digital)
- ✓ Anonymous (no personalized loyalty)
- ✓ Legal (ANSES = formal State institution)
- ✓ Higher cost/voter (USD 800–900/voter vs USD 10–50 puntero-mediated)
- ✓ No territorial control (no piquetes under Milei)

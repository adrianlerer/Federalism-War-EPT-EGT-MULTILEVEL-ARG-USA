**3. Three Mechanisms**:

- **Geographic concentration (D3)**: Concentrated benefits (high F) outcompete dispersed (high W)
- **Institutional replication**: Clientelism self-replicates (punteros → more punteros); public goods don't

- **Combined**: Selection operates on F → clientelism maximizes F → reforms must reduce F
